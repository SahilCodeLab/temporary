Happy Birthday, The Light of Patience and Guidance!

Janm Din Mubarak Ho, Hazrat Fatima Zahra (SA)

Hazrat Fatima Zahra (SA) ki roohaniat humare liye ek nayi umeed aur sabr ka paigham hai. Aapka jeevan humein hamesha ikhlas aur iman ke raaste par chalne ki salahiyat deta hai. Aaj hum unki shaan mein dua karte hain ki Allah unki roohaniyat ko humare dilon mein barhaye aur hume unki padharta par amal karne ki tofeeq de.

Special line

"Aapki shaan mein har dil se dua nikalti hai, Hazrat Fatima Zahra (SA). Aapki roohaniyat ke bina humare dil asman se bhi beqarar hote hain. Tumhare padharon se duniya roshan hoti hai, aur humein yeh yaqeen hai ki aapki dua humein har mushkil se nikalne ka suloon sikhate hain."

"Aapke zehni sabr aur iman ke saath har mushkil aasaan hoti hai. Aapki duas humein safar mein madad karti hain, aur humein yeh yaqeen hai ki tumhare irshad se humein har tarah se nur milta hai."

"Har saans, har pal, aapki barakaton se mehfooz hai. Aapki duas humein sabr aur ihsaan sikhaate hain, aur aaj ke din hum sab ki dua hai ki Allah humein bhi aapki padharta se behter amal karne ki tofeeq de."

Special Dua

O Allah! Hazrat Fatima Zahra (SA) ki shikshon aur padharta par hamare amal ko qabool farma. Humein unki sunnaton par amal karne ki tofeeq de aur humein unke irshad se munafiq rakh. Ameen.

Ameen. Ya Allah, hamein Hazrat Fatima Zahra (SA) ki irshad ke raaste par chalne ki taufiq farma. Unki roohaniyat humare dilon mein sabr aur deen ki taluqat barhayein. Ameen.

"Ya Allah! Hazrat Fatima Zahra (SA) ke padharon par humare amal ko qabool farma. Unki roohaniyat, sabr, aur ilm se humein hidayat de. Humein unke
naqsh-e-qadam par chalne ki tofeeq farma. Unki barakat se humare imaan aur ibadat ko barha de. Ameen."

Hazrat Fatima Zahra (SA) ki izzat mein humari dua mubarak ho. Allah humein unki shikshon par amal karne ki tofeeq farmaen. Ameen.
